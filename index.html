To Flowers and Many More:
A Poem

Beneath the canvas of the blue sky, in nature's realm, where spirits fly, I find refuge from life's demands, in forests, green fields, an the flower blooms of fragrant lands.

Each blade of grass, each flower bud and each towering tree, whispers secrets of delight and sets my spirit free.

With every step on the windy trail, I leave behing my worldly affairs. Amongst the trees, green and grand, I am humbled by the Earth's rich ancient lands.

The rustle of the leaves in the gentle breeze, the symphony of birds swiming in the sky, with nature's song I harmonize, a connection where I truly thrive.

The scent of flowers on green grass, with their vibrant hues, life finds place, each petal a story, in the language of blossoms, love and beauty are holy.

So, in nature's embrace, I will gladly stay, amongst the flower buds and grassy arrays, letting worry fade away, for in nature, my sould finds a way.
